About the Good(ish) Professor

Like the song says (about someone else), The Professor is straight out of Brighton, like a hip-hop commuter!

For myself, like, I imagine, many others, my first taste of the genre 'chap-hop' came from an odd little video that showed up on facebook one morning, roughly 10 years ago. In it a man of possibly ill-repute, wearing a pith-helmet like a proper sort of adventurer, rapped about tea. Just tea.

Let's take a break and watch, shall we? Brilliant!


...

There, see? That was quite alright, a spot of sunshine, yes?

From there, the Professor has conquered many a stage: voiceover work, hip-hop beefs, comic books, stage shows in sunny Tucson, AZ, the list continues.

Find him on his website, or possibly on Facebook. Heck, check him out on Twitter!
